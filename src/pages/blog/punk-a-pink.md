Punk à Pink
Night is blue
And Johny was so good

Falls are gone
Gun and cones
Bang the Doors

Fight the beast
Hit the beat
Like Céline

Bim bam boom
Captain flam
What a Prince

Feel the fear
Swear you live
Squished the Beatles

Find your life
Wish your success
Like Suzanne

Need to write
Let us know
Smoke your Bob

Swing your chord
Move your heart
Young Django
